in silence and clothe

themselves in shadows

an espionage roleplaying game

The espionage world is one behind our own; a world of wire-taps, cyber-terrorism, dead drops, spy-cameras, tricked-out cars, femme fatales, debonair superspies and plots to end or destabilize the world.

Spies are everywhere, in the shadows behind bookcases, as the sultry waitress in a restaurant, the dodgy- yet- resourceful armored car driver, the passionate diplomat, even the geek behind the high-end computer.

Deep inside you know that people are sheep.

This world is for shepherds or wolves.

Which one are you?
