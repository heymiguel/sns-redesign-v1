an espionage roleplaying game

The espionage world is one behind our own; a world of wire-taps, cyber-terrorism, dead drops, spy-cameras, tricked-out cars, femme fatales, debonair superspies and plots to end or destabilize the world.

Spies are everywhere, in the shadows behind bookcases, as the sultry waitress in a restaurant, the dodgy- yet- resourceful armored car driver, the passionate diplomat, even the geek behind the high-end computer.

Deep inside you know that people are sheep.

This world is for shepherds or wolves.

Which one are you?

Class Description

All spies are adept at gathering information, making decisions under pressure, and creating tactical assessments on the fly – with the limited information at hand. Some use their physical ability, others their natural charisma, others still live by the skin of their teeth or by the agent at their side. For a select few, information is not merely the end result of an observation or investigation; it is a weapon that is superior to any rifle or handgun, a vessel capable of traversing the vast distances on our planet in moments, and a tool capable of breaking even the most stoic poker face. They are some of the smartest people on the planet. Brilliant, observant, and dangerously cunning - Cyphers beat their opponents long before they encounter them. They use information as formidable weapons, and their sharp minds to outthink their opponents.
